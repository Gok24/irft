IRS REFUND TRACKING

Where's my refund?

The IRS sends most refunds within 3 weeks of processing your e-return. Want to know where yours is in process? Follow these steps.

How IRS refund tracking works

To check your federal tax refund status, go to the IRS' Where's My Refund tool and enter this info:

1. Enter the SSN or ITIN shown on your tax return

2. Select the filing status shown on your tax return

3. Enter the refund amount shown on your tax return

Don't forget to add 2 to 5 extra days for your bank to process the deposit after the date indicated by IRS.
